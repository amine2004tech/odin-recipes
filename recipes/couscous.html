Moroccan couscous

  • Couscous: 1 cup
  • Chicken or Vegetable Broth: 1 cup (or water)
  • Olive Oil: 2 tablespoons
  • FButter: 2 tablespoons (optional, for added richness)
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon/
  • Black Pepper: 1/4 teaspoon(or 1 can of diced tomatoes)
  • 1 cup chopped carrots
  • 1 cup chopped zucchini
  • 1 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

  1. Prepare the Couscous:

    In a medium bowl, add the couscous. Pour the hot chicken or vegetable broth over the couscous. Add the salt and pepper. Stir to combine.

  2. Add Aromatics:

    Stir in the minced garlic, cilantro, and parsley. Cook for another 2 minutes until fragrant.

  3. Vegetable Couscous:

    Saut√© the vegetables in olive oil until tender. Stir the cooked vegetables into the prepared couscous.

  4. Fluff the Couscous:

    After 5 minutes, uncover the bowl. Use a fork to fluff the couscous, breaking up any clumps. Stir in the olive oil and butter, if using. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.

  5. Serve:

    Serve the couscous warm as a side dish or base for stews and tagines.
